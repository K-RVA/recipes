
Ingredients
Meatloaf

2
    eggs, beaten 

    1/2
        cup ketchup 

        1/2
            cup milk 

            1
                cup Progresso™ Italian Style Bread Crumbs 

                1/4
                    cup finely chopped onion 

                    1
                        lb. lean ground beef 

                        Topping

                        1/4
                            cup ketchup 

                            Directions

                                1 Heat oven to 350°F. In large bowl, combine all meatloaf ingredients; mix well. Press mixture in ungreased 8x4-inch loaf pan. Bake at 350°F. for 40 minutes.
                                    2 Remove meatloaf from oven. Spread 1/4 cup ketchup evenly over top. Return to oven; bake an additional 15 to 20 minutes or until meat thermometer reaches 160°F. and beef is thoroughly cooked. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
